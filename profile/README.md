## About | 关于我们

**English**

Shanghai HiQ Smart Data Co., Ltd. is a Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) data technology company founded by a team of LCA experts and engineers. We address the market gap for rule-compliant, comprehensive, and up-to-date Chinese LCA data that aligns with global sustainability standards. Our platform provides professional LCA data resources, intelligent tools, and data services to support corporate sustainability, government policy, and academic research.

## Products | 核心产品

### HiQLCD — Chinese Life Cycle Inventory Database | 中国生命周期清单数据库

HiQLCD is a high-quality, locally developed LCA database covering 25+ Chinese industry sectors with 15,000+ datasets, including energy, raw materials, processing, transportation, and more. It is designed to support carbon footprint accounting, environmental impact assessment, EU CBAM compliance, battery passport requirements, and corporate carbon disclosure initiatives.

HiQLCD 是自主研发的高质量本土 LCA 数据库，覆盖 25+ 中国行业领域，提供 15,000+ 数据集，涵盖能源、原材料、加工工艺、运输等核心领域，支持碳足迹核算、环境影响评估、欧盟 CBAM 合规、电池护照及企业碳信息披露等应用场景。

- 📊 15,000+ datasets | 15,000+ 数据集
- 🏭 25+ Chinese industry sectors | 覆盖 25+ 中国行业
- 📋 ISO 14040/44, ISO 14067 & ILCD compliant | 符合国际标准
- 🔗 Compatible with openLCA, SimaPro & more | 兼容主流 LCA 软件
- 🔄 Regular version updates | 定期版本更新

### HiQ Cortex — AI-Powered LCA Data Workbench | AI 驱动的 LCA 数据工作台

HiQ Cortex is our AI-native data intelligence platform built on a multi-agent architecture. It brings intelligent search, dataset matching, knowledge management, and automated workflows to the LCA data ecosystem.

HiQ Cortex 是基于多智能体架构的 AI 原生数据智能平台，为 LCA 数据生态提供智能搜索、数据集匹配、知识管理和自动化工作流等能力。

- 🤖 Multi-Agent collaboration (Agent Teams) | 多智能体协作
- 🔍 Intelligent dataset search & matching | 智能数据集搜索与匹配
- 🧠 Shared knowledge base with auto-curation | 共享知识库与自动审核
- ⚡ Extensible Skills system | 可扩展的技能体系
- 📡 OpenSearch-powered retrieval | 基于 OpenSearch 的检索引擎
